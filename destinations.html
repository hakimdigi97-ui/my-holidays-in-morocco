Destinations — What Makes Each Place Unique

Cuisine, craftsmanship, landscapes, and the must-see highlights that define Morocco.

Marrakech
Palace riads, gardens, gastronomy, design & art.
Fes
Ancient medina, artisans, Andalusian heritage.
Merzouga (Erg Chebbi)
Dunes, luxury camps, stargazing.
Essaouira
Atlantic breeze, seafood, music, ramparts.
Ouarzazate
Kasbahs, film studios, gateway to Sahara.
Chefchaouen
Blue alleys, Rif trails & artisan dyes.
Casablanca
Hassan II Mosque, art-deco, ocean boulevards.
Agafay Desert
Rocky desert near Marrakech — events & stargazing.
Food to Try
  • Pastilla in Fes • Tanjia in Marrakech • Seafood in Essaouira
  • Berber tagines in Atlas villages • Street snacks in Casablanca
What to See
  • Medinas & souks • Palaces & gardens • Dunes & oases • Ocean forts
  • Film sets in Ouarzazate • Artisan workshops • Music & festivals
