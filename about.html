Our Story

A journey from the Atlas Mountains to the world, and back.

Rooted in Morocco, Shaped by the World

We are Hakim and Nouhayla, a team whose story is woven into the very fabric of Morocco. We grew up between the vibrant energy of Fes and Marrakech and the serene majesty of the Atlas Mountains. This land is not just our home; it's our heritage, our playground, and our greatest teacher.

Our journey took us to the United States for our studies, where we graduated and gained a global perspective. This experience, combined with over 10 years of extensive travel and a background as high-level athletes, gave us a unique insight. We saw the world, but our hearts always remained in Morocco.

We founded 'My Holidays in Morocco' to share the authentic beauty of our country with the world, combining our deep local knowledge with an international standard of luxury and service. We believe in travel that transforms, inspires, and connects.
